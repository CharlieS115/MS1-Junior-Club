About Junior Club Events

Part of being in Junior Club means your children are at a good level to attend camps and compete in events. Camps are a great experience as it allows your child(ren) access to a different set of instructors which will help build a more well rounded athlete and most of all they're super fun! Events have a great atmosphere and are a lot of fun. Most events will have music, food, mini competions alongside the main event, prizes up for grabs and a great day or night with friends!
